How to register as a creator on Fahdu?

Please note that all accounts on Fahdu are first created as a “user account”. To convert your existing account into a creator account, follow these steps:
  • Visit the Fahdu website and click on the “Sign Up” button
  • Provide an email address and set a password. Do all the necessary steps as prompted
  • If you already have a user account, simply log in by providing your registered email and password
  • Now, to convert into a “creator account” click on the three lines on the top right corner of your profile
  • A drop down menu will appear. Click on settings and you will see a list of settings appear on the left side of the screen
  • Click on “Get Verified” and follow all the instructions as prompted
  • Once done, you will have access to the creator dashboard. Now you can connect to your audience, post & manage content and eventually earn money on Fahdu
